About This Location

Where students can get administrative help. The Admin Building serves as the central hub for all administrative services at Boston International College. This facility houses various departments that provide essential support services to students, faculty, and staff.

From enrollment and registration to student records and financial services, the Admin Building is your one-stop destination for all administrative needs. The building is designed to provide efficient and friendly service to the college community.

Services Available

  • Fast problem solving facilities
  • Comprehensive IT support services
  • ID card renewal and creation support
  • Student registration and enrollment
  • Academic records management
  • Financial aid and billing services
  • Student information updates
  • Document verification and certification

Office Hours & Contact

The Admin Building operates during regular business hours to serve students and staff. Our dedicated administrative team is committed to providing quick and efficient solutions to all your academic and administrative needs. For urgent matters, emergency contact information is available at the reception desk.
