BIC Startup Innovation Space

About This Location

A cozy area for students to relax and socialize. The BIC Startup space is designed to foster innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurial thinking among students. This modern facility provides a collaborative environment where ideas can flourish and turn into reality.

This space serves as an incubator for student-led initiatives, startup ideas, and collaborative projects. It's where the next generation of entrepreneurs gather to brainstorm, network, and develop their business concepts.

Features & Amenities

  • Comfortable seating arrangements
  • Multiple charging stations for devices
  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Collaborative workspaces
  • Whiteboard walls for brainstorming
  • Presentation equipment
  • Coffee and refreshment area
  • Quiet zones for focused work

Innovation Activities

This is the perfect place for students to brainstorm ideas and develop innovative solutions. The space regularly hosts startup workshops, pitch competitions, mentorship sessions, and networking events. Students can access resources for business plan development, market research, and prototype creation.
